ABOUT US

The Companies History

Founded in 2009 by Mufaddal Dahodwala, ICE India is a full service corporate event planning and travel management company. We've steadily carved out a niche within the meetings, incentives, conferences, events (MICE) segment in the Indian event management landscape. Based in Mumbai, our team is proficient in managing domestic & international travel incentives, business conferences & corporate events for employees and channel partners (dealers or distributors).
